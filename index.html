About Us

What is SNIFF?

SNIFF is more than just a meme coin with a catchy name. SNIFF provide a contract scanner for any contracts deploying on SHIBARIUM. It also aims to revolutionize the way investors discover, analyze, and participate in new projects through its integrated launchpad utility on SHIBARIUM. By providing a platform for up-and-coming projects to showcase their potential, SNIFF empowers its community members with the opportunity to identify and invest in promising ventures at their earliest stages.

The SNIFF launchpad is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, allowing both experienced investors and newcomers to the crypto space to participate in a secure and streamlined manner. Through its vetting process and strong focus on transparency, the SNIFF launchpad ensures that only projects with genuine potential and credibility are featured, fostering trust and confidence among its users.

As a unique blend of meme token and launchpad utility, SNIFF has positioned itself as a pioneering force on the Shibarium Layer-2 Chain

How to buy?

STEP 1

✔️ Download the MetaMask Chrome Extension or mobile app.

✔️ Setup your account & add the Sniff to your network list.

STEP 2

✔️ Use another exchange to purchase ETH and send it over to your MetaMask Wallet Address.

✔️ Or if you already have ETH, simply send it to your MetaMask Wallet.

STEP 3

✔️ Head on over to uniswap and use the Contract Address to find the Token. Set the slippage tolerance to 0.01% - 0.1% (but could be higher depending on how much demand there is).
